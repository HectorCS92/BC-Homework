Wind Speed

_______________________________________________________________________________
As expected, the wind speed becomes significantly faster as one approaches the equator. More interestingly, however, is the fact that the southern hemisphere tends to be more windy this time of the year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the Earth at the time of the year this data was gathered.
